Medical Guidelines

What MomCare Guide Covers

MomCare Guide is a mobile app made for pregnant Filipino women and their partners in Sta. Mesa, Manila. It gives trusted, easy-to-understand maternal health information that fits local culture and daily life.

We focus on:

  • Pregnancy tracking – keep track of your pregnancy journey
  • Filipino maternal recipes – safe, affordable, local Filipino recipes
  • Appointment reminders – never miss a check-up!
  • Library maternal resources – maternal health articles and guides
  • Community forum – connect with other parents
  • Emergency SOS button – instant help during emergencies
Emergency SOS: Enables call and sends SMS to your trusted emergency contacts, along with 911 as default contact, and shares your location.

During sign-up, you’ll agree to let the app send you notifications for features to work, SMS, and location for emergencies. You’ll also be asked to allow these permissions when you first open the app.

Teleconsultation (Call Support)

In the Help & Support section, you can call our partner health hotline for maternal health advice and other concerns from trained professional.

Important Limitations

MomCare Guide is not a hospital or doctor. It gives information and support — not medical diagnosis or treatment.

Please understand these limits:

  • Emergency SOS needs mobile data to share your location. If mobile data/Internet access is off, this feature will not work.
  • Your emergency contacts must have the correct contact number to be able to receive your SMS.
  • Content is made for Filipino moms — may not apply to other countries or cultures.
  • Teleconsultation uses landline calls — may cost depending on your mobile plan and carrier.
  • Teleconsultation has set hours — no 24/7 live support.
  • No real-time video doctor visits or advanced diagnostics.
For serious symptoms: Go to your nearest maternal clinic or call 911 immediately. Do not wait for app response.

Who Should Use This App?

Best for: Filipino pregnant women and partners who want reliable, trusted maternal health resources and emergency support.

Not for: Complex/unusual diagnosis, or replacing doctor visits.

MomCare Guide is a supportive tool, not a substitute for professional medical care. Always consult a doctor for health concerns.
