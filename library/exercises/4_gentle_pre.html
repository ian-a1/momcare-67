Interval Walking
  1. Alternate 2 minutes of moderate walking with 1 minute of slow walking for 15-20 minutes on flat surfaces
Benefits:[19]
Improves cardiovascular endurance
Enhances mood and energy levels
Supports weight management safely
Notes:
Consult a doctor before starting
Use a timer to track intervals
Stay hydrated and avoid overexertion

Improves circulation and muscle tone
