Pelvic Tilt
  1. Start in a tabletop position
  2. Put hands underneath your shoulders
  3. Put knees underneath your hips
  4. Tilt pelvis back and forth while engaging core, holding 5-10 seconds each way
Benefits:[3][4][24]
Relieves back/sciatic pain
Strengthens abdominals
Supports postpartum recovery
Chin to Chest Stretch
  1. Sit tall
  2. Drop chin to chest
  3. Hold 15-30 seconds
  4. Then rotate head side-to-side
Benefits:[25]
Eases neck tension
Promotes relaxation post-birth
