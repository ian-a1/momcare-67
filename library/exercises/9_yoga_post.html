Cat-Cow Pose
  1. Similar to prenatal version
  2. Start on hands and knees
  3. Gently flow between arching and rounding your back
  4. Focus on releasing back tension and rebuilding core strength

Child's Pose
  1. Similar to prenatal version
  2. Kneel on the floor
  3. Fold forward with arms extended
  4. Use pillows for support as needed
Benefits:[4][7]
Promotes relaxation
Eases muscle soreness
Reduces depression symptoms
Legs-Up-the-Wall Pose
  1. Lie on your back near a wall
  2. Elevate your legs against the wall
  3. Keep arms relaxed by your sides
  4. Hold for 5-15 minutes
Benefits:[4]
Reduces swelling
