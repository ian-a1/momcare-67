Marching in Place
  1. Stand tall
  2. Lift your knees to hip height while swinging arms, alternatingly
  3. Do for 10-15 minutes
Benefits:[1][18]
Builds cardio endurance
Improves circulation
Manages weight gain
Arm Circles
  1. Start in a standing position with arms raised in a T-position
  2. Gently swing arms in a circling motion
  3. Start with small circling motions first, then larger
  4. Do for 5-10 minutes
Benefits:[1][18]
Enhances upper body mobility
Boosts heart rate gently
Modified Jumping Jacks
  1. Step one foot out while raising arms overhead
  2. Alternate sides using your foot without jumping
  3. Do for 10 minutes
Benefits:[1][18]
Improves mood
Reduces stress
Safe, low-impact cardio
