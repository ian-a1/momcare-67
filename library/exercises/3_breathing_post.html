üí® Breathing Exercise Examples

Diaphragmatic (Belly) Breathing
  1. Similar to antenatal
  2. Focus on belly expansion on inhale (4 counts) and full exhale (4-6 counts)
Benefits:[12]
Aids relaxation
Reduces postpartum stress
Supports pelvic floor healing
360-Degree Breathing
  1. Inhale to expand the ribcage in all directions (front, sides, back)
  2. Exhale to engage the core gently
Benefits:[16]
Helps heal diastasis recti
Coordinates breathing with pelvic floor for incontinence prevention
Mindful/Focused Breathing
  1. Sit comfortably
  2. Inhale through nose focusing on present sensations
  3. Exhale releasing tension
Benefits:[17]
Reduces anxiety and promotes calm
Significant part of postnatal relaxation techniques
