Torso Rotation
  1. Sit cross-legged on the floor
  2. Hold right foot with left hand
  3. Gently rotate upper body to the right, holding 20-30 seconds
  4. Repeat on the other side
Benefits:[5][24]
Relieves back and upper torso tension
Eases pregnancy-related aches
Neck and Shoulder Stretch
  1. Sit tall
  2. Bring chin to chest
  3. Tilt head side-to-side, holding 15-30 seconds each side
Benefits:[5][24]
Alleviates shoulder tension
Promotes relaxation during pregnancy
Seated Hamstring Stretch
  1. Sit on the edge of a chair
  2. Extend one leg forward with heel on floor
  3. Lean slightly forward from hips, keeping back straight
Benefits:[23]
Stretches hamstrings and lower back
Helps with pelvic girdle pain
