Cat-Cow Pose
  1. Start on hands and knees position
  2. Alternate arching (cow: inhale, drop belly, lift head)
  3. And rounding (cat: exhale, tuck chin, arch back)
  4. Do for at least 5-10 breaths
Benefits:[12][26]
Eases back pain
Improves flexibility
Prepares for labor
Child's Pose
  1. Kneel on the floor
  2. Sit back on heels
  3. Extend arms forward
  4. Rest forehead down
  5. Use pillow or a soft pad for belly support
Benefits:[12][27]
Reduces stress
Stretches hips and back
Promotes relaxation
Goddess Pose
  1. Stand with your feet wide
  2. Toes pointed out
  3. Bend knees into a squat
  4. Hands at heart or overhead
Benefits:[12]
Builds leg strength
Opens hips
Enhances endurance for delivery
Warrior II (Two)
  1. Wide stance
  2. Front knee bent over ankle
  3. Arms extended parallel to floor
  4. Gaze over the front hand
Benefits:[12][28]
Boosts stability
Strengthens legs
Reduces anxiety
