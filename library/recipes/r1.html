üë©‚Äçüç≥ Cooking Instructions

  1. Heat cooking oil in a cooking pot over medium heat. Add the garlic, onion, and ginger to the pot. Saut√© until the onion becomes soft and translucent, about 3-5 minutes.
  2. Add the chicken pieces to the pot with the aromatics. Cook for about 2 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the chicken turns light brown on all sides. Ensure all pieces are evenly browned.
  3. Pour in 2 tablespoons of fish sauce and stir well to coat the chicken. Add the rice wash to the pot and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low and let it simmer for 10 minutes. Add water as needed to adjust the stew's consistency and bring to a boil again.
  4. Add the green papaya wedges to the pot. Continue to simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the papaya is tender when pierced with a fork.
  5. Add the Maggi Magic Chicken Cubes and stir until dissolved. Season with ground black pepper to taste (optional). Turn off the heat. Add the hot pepper leaves and malunggay leaves to the pot. Cover the pot for 2 minutes to allow the residual heat to cook the greens.
  6. Transfer to a serving bowl and serve hot. Share and enjoy!
